About

Dr. Nipun Jain completed MBBS in 1996 from V.S.S Medical College, Sambalpur, Orissa, Sambalpur University; MD from S.N Medical College, Agra University, Uttar Pradesh in 2002, worked as Senior Resident in D.D.U Hospital for 3 years where he was exposed to all kind of Skin, Hair, Nail disorders, Sexually transmitted diseases and cosmetic treatments. Also worked as a Senior Consultant Dermatologist at Columbia-Asia Hospital, Gurgaon. Currently working at Sri Balaji Action Hospital, New Delhi since 2007. Dr Nipun Jain is a certified Botox Specialist and deals in all kind of cosmetic procedures like Platelet Rich Plasma and Lasers.
